kleśajñeyāvṛtestasmāt prahāṇārthamaśeṣataḥ|prajñāpāramitāṃ yogī sopāyaṃ bhāvayet sadā

upāyarahitā prajñā'pyupāyaḥ prajñayā vinā|yato bandha iti proktau praheyaṃ nobhayaṃ tataḥ

kā prajñā ka upāyaśca śaṅkāmiti nirāsitum|upāyasya ca prajñāyāḥ bhedaḥ samyak prakāśyate

prajñāpāramitāṃ tyaktvā dānapāramitādayaḥ|sarve hi kuśalāḥ dharmāḥ upāyāḥ jinabhāṣitāḥ

upāyābhyāsavaśyātmā yo hi prajñāṃ vibhāvayet|śīghraṃ sa labhate bodhiṃ na nairātmyaikabhāvanāt

skandhāyatanadhātūnāmanutpādāvabodhinām|svabhāvaśūnyatājñānaṃ prajñeti parikīrtitā
